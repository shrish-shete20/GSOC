Abstract:

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus. This project aims to analyse the key causes that are affecting COVID 19 cases without affecting it directly. We intend to present a comprehensive overview of the factors affecting COVID 19 all over the world and some thought-provoking questions on the management of individuals with a diagnosis of COVID-19.Finally, the R SHINY APP present and summarize the data in a interactive dashboard.

Introduction :

The COVID-19 pandemic has led loss of so many lives worldwide and has presented an unprecedented challenge to all the governments and to various sectors, mainly health line workers working daily to reduce the human loss as much as possible. Two years ago, the whole world had come to a stand still and we all know the reason Covid-19. There were different number of waves in each countries and different variants of covid affecting each country in different ways. For every country there are a few indicators which tell us about it. And every country has a different story altogether. We collected the indicators which could possibly have had impact on controlling covid.

The Indicators we have considered are:

Conclusions :

-> The countries with higher GDP are the countries which have relatively lesser death rate when compared to the countries with lesser GDP. The countries with higher GDP are the countries with relatively lesser population and are able to conduct more number of tests per population compared to the countries with higher population (which has relatively lesser GDP).

-> Hence, we can conclude that countries with higher GDP are economically stable and medically advanced, therefore they are able to provide better facilities.

-> From the above plots we can come to a conclusion that population size indeed had an effect on both number of covid cases and deaths due to covid.

-> The most exposed and effected age group due to covid is people of age greater than 65, since their deaths contribute to the highest percentage(i.e.66%) of total number of deaths.

-> Countries with Life Expectancy higher than the Average Life Expectancy has comparatively lower death rate due to Covid19.

-> Countries with darker Color in the Interactive world map depicts that those countries were either economically more stable or medically advanced that they could start the Vaccination drive so earlier.
